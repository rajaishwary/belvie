Why drink Belvie?


Belvie L’Eau Minerale Naturelle is sourced from 200 Ft. below the ground from an aquifier untouched by human activity, to give you water that is rich in minerals, fresh and natural.
Belvie adds more value to its mineral water by enforcing strict quality standards on its water:
- With its unique 9 stage quality procedure, Belvie ensures that every drop of water served is consistently pure.
- Belvie’s 9 stage quality process comprises of modern Ultra Violet and Ozonization technology that kills kills the harmful bacteriological matter found in water from all
sources, which is why our water is 100% safe and healthy.
- BELVIE L’EAU MINERALE NATURELLE IS THE ONLY WATER IN NIGER WITH A PH VALUE OF 7-7.2, CONSIDERED THE MOST HEALTHY FOR THE BODY.
- Belvie has the most modern in house chemical and micro biological labs to index every batch that we bottle, as a part of our commitment to offer guaranteed purity.
Now you can always drink water that is natural, fresh, healthy and pure! Drink Belvie: Purity in every drop.
